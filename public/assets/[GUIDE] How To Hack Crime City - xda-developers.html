How To Hack Crime City

- GameCIH
Don't Have GameGuardian? Download It From HERE

Don't Have GameCIH? Download It From HERE

To Get Money Quckly, Follow The Given Steps:

Install GameGuardian



Open GameGuardian



Now Open Crime City



Tap & Hold The GameGuardian Icon



Now Keep On Clicking The Right Arrow Until The Speed Increases To "1000"



Now You Can Keep On Collecting Money Much Much Faster

To Get Unlimited Energy, Follow The Given Steps:

Open GameCIH

Open Crime City

Click On "Input Name" On GameCIH

Type In "mEnergyRegen"

Now You Will Get 2 Things:
- mEnergyRegenerationTimeSecs
- mEnergyRegenerationAmount

mEnergyRegenerationTimeSecs Means How Much Time (Seconds) It Will Take To Refill Some Amount Of Energy

mEnergyRegenerationAmount Means How Much Energy Will Be Refilled After The Above ↑ Time

Change "mEnergyRegenerationTimeSecs" To 1 (Second)

Change "mEnergyRegenerationAmount" To The Maximum Amount Of Energy You Have

So Now After Every 1 Second, Your Energy Will Get Fully Refilled

Open GameCIH

Open Crime City

Click On "Input Name" On GameCIH

Type In "mStaminaRegen"

Now You Will Get 2 Things:
- mStaminaRegenerationTimeSecs
- mStaminaRegenerationAmount

mStaminaRegenerationTimeSecs Means How Much Time (Seconds) It Will Take To Refill Some Amount Of Stamina

mStaminaRegenerationAmount Means How Much Stamina Will Be Refilled After The Above ↑ Time
